✨ Task 5: Magazine Cover – "Feminine Way"

Name: Abilashiny Selvakumaran
Batch: February-P1 2026
Domain: Graphic Design Intern

🎯 Objective

Design a magazine cover that celebrates confidence, individuality, and the strength of femininity, while exploring editorial design and visual storytelling.

🛠 Steps Performed

Conceptualization: Planned a concept reflecting strength and elegance.

Color & Typography Selection: Chose soft pastel tones and bold typography for balance.

Design Creation: Used Canva to create a split-tone effect and highlight focal points.

Visual Storytelling: Arranged elements to convey focus, ambition, and self-expression.

Final Export: Exported magazine cover as PNG and PDF for portfolio and submission.

🖌 Tools Used

Canva

✅ Outcome / Result

A visually engaging magazine cover that reflects feminine creativity and empowerment.

Balanced use of typography, pastel tones, and visual hierarchy.

Ready for portfolio display and professional presentation.
